Maltese Law — Subletting rules
Subletting is only permitted with the landlord's written consent. RestMalta generates the sub-lease agreement and the landlord consent document automatically.
🏛️
Housing Authority registration — tenant's responsibility
RestMalta's role ends with the signed sub-lease. It is the tenant's legal obligation to register the sub-lease with the Housing Authority of Malta. RestMalta is not responsible for this registration. For more information: housingauthority.org.mt
